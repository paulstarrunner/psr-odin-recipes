Lasagna

Description

The All Father prefers his lasagna with the following ingredients

  • Cremete lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Oregano, salt, pepper, thyme, and garlic
  • Onions (diced)
  • Ground beef

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sweat the onions
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until brown
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and spices
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Layer the lasagna pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
