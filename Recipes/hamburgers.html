Hamburgers

Description

Odin's favorite hamburger recipe includes the following ingredients.

  • Hamburger Bun
  • Burger Patty
  • Lettuce, Tomato, Onion
  • Papa Odin's Special Sauce

Instructions

  1. Toast the hamburger bun
  2. Cook the burger patty
  3. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and Papa Odin's Special Sauce
