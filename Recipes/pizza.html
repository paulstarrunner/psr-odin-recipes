Hamburgers

Description

While Odin isn't the biggest fan of pizza his raven is and likes them with the following ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • 1 can of Chef Boyardee Pizza Sauce
  • Oregano, thyme, salt, pepper
  • Pineapple
  • Bacon
  • Ground Beef
  • Black Olives
  • Shredded Mozzorella Cheese

Instructions

  1. Layer the sauce, toppings and cheese on the crust
  2. Place in oven on a pizza stone and bake at 550 for 20 mins
  3. Great Odin's raven! We forgot the ranch for dipping!
